The Toolkit

Six question types. Each one targets a different psychological lever. No overlap, no filler.

Why not just ask your model to write questions?

You can. They'll be decent. But decent questions get decent answers. Here's why that's not enough.

🛡️

Models optimize for safety, not signal

LLMs are trained to be helpful and non-threatening. Great questions are slightly uncomfortable. A model will never ask "What's the most expensive thing you own that you never use?" unprompted — it's too risky. BetterAsk's archetypes push past the safety reflex into territory that actually reveals something.

📏

No model can tell a 3 from an 8

Without a quality framework, your model generates "sounds deep" slop and can't tell the difference. BetterAsk scores every question on 6 weighted dimensions — Surprise, Specificity, Conversation Fuel, Self-Revelation, Fun Factor, Universality. You get a number, not a guess.

🧬

607 questions tested on real humans

Models generate from patterns. Our corpus comes from END SMALL TALK — 607 questions tested in real conversations, at real bars, on real dates, in real interviews. That's training data no model has. Every archetype, every example, every scoring weight comes from what actually worked face-to-face.

🔄

Archetype rotation prevents pattern collapse

Ask a model for 5 questions and you'll get 5 variations of the same pattern. BetterAsk rotates across 6 distinct psychological archetypes — each one targets a different lever (specificity, shared truth, values, reframe, courage, aspiration). Your users never feel like they're answering the same question twice.

📈

Machine learning makes it smarter every day

Every question scored through BetterAsk feeds back into the calibration engine. Human raters score questions 1-10 across all six dimensions. Those scores adjust archetype weights, refine context selection, and sharpen the scoring rubric itself. The corpus grows. The weights tighten. The gap between BetterAsk and raw model output widens every single day. Our goal is simple: become the world's best question-generating system — not by being the biggest model, but by having the most rigorously calibrated dataset of what makes a question actually work on a real human being.

The honest pitch:

"Your model can generate questions. BetterAsk tells it which questions to generate, why, and whether they're any good."

API Endpoints

POST/generate

Generate questions using archetypes. Returns LLM prompts you pass to your model.

{
  "context": "onboarding",
  "about": "what motivates this person at work",
  "depth": "medium",
  "archetype": "auto",
  "count": 3,
  "avoid": ["politics"]
}
POST/score

Score any question against the EST rubric (6 dimensions, weighted composite).

{
  "question": "If you repeated yesterday 365 times, where would you be?"
}
GET/archetypes

List all 6 archetypes with descriptions, patterns, examples, and signals.

GET/health

Service health check — returns status, corpus size, version.
